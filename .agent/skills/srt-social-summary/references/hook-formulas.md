# Hook Formulas and Copywriting Templates

Proven hook formulas and copywriting frameworks for creating attention-grabbing social media content.

## The Psychology of Hooks

Effective hooks tap into:
- **Curiosity**: Create information gaps
- **Urgency**: Time-sensitive or limited information
- **Relevance**: Directly addresses audience pain points
- **Surprise**: Unexpected facts or perspectives
- **Emotion**: Triggers feelings (fear, joy, anger, hope)
- **Specificity**: Concrete numbers and details

---

## Hook Formula Categories

### 1. Question Hooks

**Pattern**: Ask a question that your audience wants answered

**Templates**:
- "What if I told you [surprising fact]?"
- "Ever wondered why [common phenomenon]?"
- "Are you making this [mistake] without realizing it?"
- "What's the difference between [A] and [B]?"
- "Why do [group] always [action]?"

**Examples**:
- "What if I told you 90% of productivity advice is wrong?"
- "Ever wondered why some people achieve more in 4 hours than others do in 8?"
- "Are you sabotaging your growth without realizing it?"

**Best for**: Facebook, Thread
**Engagement type**: Comments, discussion

---

### 2. Number Hooks

**Pattern**: Use specific numbers to promise clear value

**Templates**:
- "[Number] ways to [achieve desired outcome]"
- "[Number] mistakes [audience] make with [topic]"
- "The [number]-step framework for [result]"
- "[Number] secrets [experts] don't want you to know"
- "From [bad state] to [good state] in [number] days"

**Examples**:
- "5 ways to double your productivity without working harder"
- "3 mistakes beginners make with social media marketing"
- "The 4-step framework for viral content"
- "From burnout to balance in 30 days"

**Best for**: All platforms
**Engagement type**: Saves, shares

---

### 3. Contrarian Hooks

**Pattern**: Challenge conventional wisdom

**Templates**:
- "Stop doing [common advice]. Here's what actually works:"
- "Unpopular opinion: [controversial take]"
- "Everyone says [common belief]. They're wrong."
- "The truth about [topic] that nobody talks about"
- "Why [popular method] is actually holding you back"

**Examples**:
- "Stop setting goals. Here's what actually works:"
- "Unpopular opinion: Hustle culture is killing your creativity"
- "Everyone says 'follow your passion.' They're wrong."
- "Why morning routines are actually holding you back"

**Best for**: Thread, Facebook
**Engagement type**: Debates, shares

---

### 4. Transformation Hooks

**Pattern**: Show before/after or promise transformation

**Templates**:
- "How I went from [bad state] to [good state] in [timeframe]"
- "The [method] that changed everything for me"
- "Before I knew [insight], I was [struggling]. Now I'm [thriving]."
- "I used to [bad habit]. Then I discovered [solution]."
- "From [starting point] to [achievement]: My journey"

**Examples**:
- "How I went from 0 to 10K followers in 90 days"
- "The mindset shift that changed everything for me"
- "Before I knew this, I was broke. Now I'm financially free."
- "I used to work 80-hour weeks. Then I discovered time-blocking."

**Best for**: Facebook, YouTube
**Engagement type**: Inspiration, saves

---

### 5. Problem-Solution Hooks

**Pattern**: Identify pain point, promise solution

**Templates**:
- "Struggling with [problem]? Here's the fix:"
- "If you're [experiencing pain point], you need to know this"
- "[Problem] doesn't have to be hard. Here's the simple solution:"
- "The real reason you're [struggling] (and how to fix it)"
- "Tired of [frustration]? Try this instead:"

**Examples**:
- "Struggling with writer's block? Here's the fix:"
- "If you're burning out, you need to know this"
- "Marketing doesn't have to be hard. Here's the simple solution:"
- "Tired of feeling overwhelmed? Try this instead:"

**Best for**: All platforms
**Engagement type**: Saves, comments

---

### 6. Curiosity Gap Hooks

**Pattern**: Tease valuable information without revealing it

**Templates**:
- "The one thing [successful people] do that you don't"
- "This [simple thing] changed my entire [area of life]"
- "Nobody talks about this, but it's the key to [success]"
- "The [adjective] secret to [desired outcome]"
- "What [experts] know that you don't (yet)"

**Examples**:
- "The one thing successful creators do that you don't"
- "This 5-minute habit changed my entire career"
- "Nobody talks about this, but it's the key to consistency"
- "The counterintuitive secret to going viral"

**Best for**: Thread, Facebook
**Engagement type**: Clicks, engagement

---

### 7. Urgency/FOMO Hooks

**Pattern**: Create fear of missing out or time pressure

**Templates**:
- "If you're not doing [action], you're already behind"
- "This is your sign to [take action]"
- "Don't make the same mistake I did with [topic]"
- "Last chance to [opportunity] before [deadline/change]"
- "While you're [wasting time], others are [succeeding]"

**Examples**:
- "If you're not building your personal brand, you're already behind"
- "This is your sign to start that project"
- "Don't make the same mistake I did with my first product launch"
- "While you're overthinking, others are executing"

**Best for**: Thread, Facebook
**Engagement type**: Immediate action

---

### 8. Authority/Credibility Hooks

**Pattern**: Establish expertise or share insider knowledge

**Templates**:
- "After [number] years of [experience], here's what I learned:"
- "I've [impressive achievement]. Here's my #1 tip:"
- "As a [expert role], I can tell you [insight]"
- "[Number] [experts] agree: [statement]"
- "I analyzed [large number] [things] and found [pattern]"

**Examples**:
- "After 10 years of marketing, here's what I learned:"
- "I've built 3 six-figure businesses. Here's my #1 tip:"
- "As a therapist, I can tell you: burnout is preventable"
- "I analyzed 1,000 viral posts and found this pattern"

**Best for**: YouTube, Facebook
**Engagement type**: Trust, saves

---

### 9. Story Hooks

**Pattern**: Start with a compelling narrative moment

**Templates**:
- "I'll never forget the day [pivotal moment]"
- "Three years ago, I was [bad situation]. Today, [success]."
- "The moment I realized [insight] changed everything"
- "I almost gave up on [goal]. Then this happened:"
- "Picture this: [vivid scene description]"

**Examples**:
- "I'll never forget the day I lost my job and found my purpose"
- "Three years ago, I was $50K in debt. Today, I'm debt-free."
- "The moment I realized hard work ≠ results changed everything"
- "Picture this: 3 AM, crying over my laptop, ready to quit..."

**Best for**: Facebook, YouTube
**Engagement type**: Emotional connection

---

### 10. Myth-Busting Hooks

**Pattern**: Debunk common misconceptions

**Templates**:
- "The biggest lie about [topic] is [myth]"
- "Think [common belief]? Think again."
- "Myth: [false belief]. Reality: [truth]."
- "Let's bust the myth that [misconception]"
- "You've been told [lie]. Here's the truth:"

**Examples**:
- "The biggest lie about success is that it requires sacrifice"
- "Think you need 10K followers to make money? Think again."
- "Myth: You need to post daily. Reality: Quality > Quantity."
- "You've been told to 'fake it till you make it.' Here's the truth:"

**Best for**: Thread, Facebook
**Engagement type**: Shares, debates

## Platform-Specific Hook Strategies

### Facebook Hooks

**Characteristics**:
- Longer hooks acceptable (2-3 sentences)
- Emotional resonance crucial
- Community-focused language
- Conversational tone

**Top Performers**:
1. Story hooks
2. Question hooks
3. Transformation hooks

**Example**:
```
Remember when you thought success meant working 80-hour weeks? 

I did too. Until I discovered this simple framework that helped me achieve more in 4 hours than I used to in full days.

Here's what changed everything... 👇
```

---

### Thread Hooks

**Characteristics**:
- Ultra-concise (first 5 words critical)
- Punchy and direct
- Often controversial or bold
- Standalone value

**Top Performers**:
1. Contrarian hooks
2. Number hooks
3. Curiosity gap hooks

**Example**:
```
Stop setting goals.

Seriously.

Here's what actually works:

[Thread continues...]
```

---

### YouTube Hooks

**Characteristics**:
- SEO-optimized with keywords
- Clear value proposition
- Longer format acceptable
- Informative tone

**Top Performers**:
1. Number hooks
2. Problem-solution hooks
3. Authority hooks

**Example**:
```
5 Productivity Hacks That Tripled My Output (Without Burning Out)

If you're working harder but not seeing results, this video will change everything. I'm sharing the exact framework I used to go from overwhelmed and unproductive to achieving more in less time—without sacrificing my health or relationships.

[Description continues...]
```

## Advanced Hook Techniques

### The Pattern Interrupt

Break expected patterns to grab attention:
- Start with "No." or "Stop."
- Use unexpected formatting
- Contradict yourself intentionally
- Use unusual punctuation

**Example**:
```
No.

You don't need another productivity app.

You need this instead:
```

---

### The Specificity Principle

Replace vague terms with specific details:

❌ Vague: "I made a lot of money"
✅ Specific: "I made $47,392 in 90 days"

❌ Vague: "This changed my life"
✅ Specific: "This 5-minute morning habit eliminated my anxiety"

❌ Vague: "Many experts agree"
✅ Specific: "127 marketing professionals surveyed said"

---

### The Relatability Factor

Make hooks personally relevant:

**Templates**:
- "If you're a [specific role], you need to hear this"
- "For everyone who's ever [common experience]"
- "Calling all [specific group]:"
- "This is for the [personality type] who [behavior]"

**Examples**:
- "If you're a solopreneur drowning in tasks, you need to hear this"
- "For everyone who's ever felt like an imposter"
- "Calling all perfectionists who can't finish projects:"
- "This is for the overachiever who's secretly burned out"

---

### The Emotional Amplifier

Intensify emotional impact with power words:

**Power Words by Emotion**:

**Curiosity**: Secret, hidden, revealed, exposed, untold, forbidden
**Urgency**: Now, today, immediately, before, deadline, last chance
**Fear**: Mistake, danger, warning, avoid, never, worst
**Desire**: Ultimate, perfect, ideal, dream, best, proven
**Trust**: Guaranteed, certified, verified, tested, backed, authentic

**Example Transformation**:
❌ Weak: "Ways to improve your writing"
✅ Strong: "The secret formula for irresistible writing (revealed)"

## Hook Testing Framework

### Elements to Test

1. **Opening word**: Question vs. statement vs. number
2. **Length**: Short (5-10 words) vs. medium (11-20) vs. long (20+)
3. **Tone**: Casual vs. professional vs. provocative
4. **Specificity**: Vague vs. specific numbers/details
5. **Emotion**: Which emotion to trigger

### A/B Testing Template

Create variations of the same hook:

**Version A** (Question):
"Are you making these 3 fatal marketing mistakes?"

**Version B** (Contrarian):
"Stop following this marketing advice. It's killing your growth."

**Version C** (Number):
"3 marketing mistakes that cost me $10K (and how to avoid them)"

**Version D** (Story):
"I lost $10K before I learned these 3 marketing lessons"

Test and measure:
- Click-through rate
- Engagement rate
- Share rate
- Comment quality

## Hook Writing Process

### Step 1: Identify the Core Value

What's the ONE thing your audience will gain?
- Knowledge?
- Transformation?
- Entertainment?
- Solution to problem?

### Step 2: Know Your Audience's Pain Points

What keeps them up at night?
- Fears?
- Frustrations?
- Desires?
- Challenges?

### Step 3: Choose Your Hook Type

Based on content and platform:
- Educational → Number or Problem-Solution
- Inspirational → Transformation or Story
- Controversial → Contrarian or Myth-Busting
- Entertaining → Curiosity Gap or Question

### Step 4: Write 5 Variations

Don't settle for the first hook. Write:
1. Question version
2. Number version
3. Contrarian version
4. Story version
5. Curiosity gap version

### Step 5: Apply the 5-Second Test

Read your hook and ask:
- Would I stop scrolling?
- Is the value clear?
- Does it create curiosity?
- Is it specific enough?
- Does it match platform tone?

### Step 6: Refine and Test

- Remove unnecessary words
- Add power words
- Increase specificity
- Test with target audience
- Iterate based on performance

## Common Hook Mistakes to Avoid

### ❌ Mistake #1: Being Too Vague

**Bad**: "Tips for better productivity"
**Good**: "5 productivity hacks that saved me 15 hours/week"

### ❌ Mistake #2: Burying the Lead

**Bad**: "I've been thinking a lot about productivity lately, and after years of trial and error, I've discovered..."
**Good**: "This 5-minute habit tripled my productivity"

### ❌ Mistake #3: Clickbait Without Delivery

**Bad**: "You won't BELIEVE what happened next!!!"
**Good**: "The unexpected result of posting daily for 30 days"

### ❌ Mistake #4: No Emotional Hook

**Bad**: "Here are some marketing statistics"
**Good**: "These marketing stats will change how you create content"

### ❌ Mistake #5: Wrong Platform Tone

**Bad** (Thread): "I've been reflecting on my journey and wanted to share some thoughts with you all about the importance of..."
**Good** (Thread): "Your content isn't failing. Your distribution is."

## Final Hook Writing Tips

1. **Write hooks last**: Create content first, then craft the perfect hook
2. **Use the "scroll test"**: Would you stop scrolling for this?
3. **Read aloud**: Does it sound natural and compelling?
4. **Check for clarity**: Is the value immediately obvious?
5. **Match platform energy**: Adjust tone for each platform
6. **Test and iterate**: Track what works, do more of it
7. **Study top performers**: Analyze viral content in your niche
8. **Keep a swipe file**: Save hooks that grab your attention
9. **Be authentic**: Don't promise what you can't deliver
10. **Practice daily**: Hook writing is a skill that improves with repetition

Remember: The best hook is one that delivers on its promise and provides genuine value to your audience.
